Module Overview

Paralegals play a crucial role in the legal system by providing essential support to lawyers and legal teams. In this module, you will learn about the fundamental responsibilities of a paralegal, including legal research, document preparation, client interaction, and case management.

Throughout this course, you'll develop practical skills that are directly applicable to real-world legal environments. You'll understand the ethical considerations that govern paralegal work and learn how to maintain professional standards in all aspects of your role.

Key Topics Covered:
  • Role and responsibilities of a paralegal
  • Legal terminology and documentation
  • Research methods and legal databases
  • Client confidentiality and ethics
  • Case management procedures
